INSTRUCTIONS: Auto-Configuration for a Specified Illumination Area

1. Insert the desired dimensions into the Illumination Area fields.

2. Select a power supply Derate factor, if desired. (Default is 10%)

3. View the System Parameters and Components Required.

4. Switch to Manual Configuration Mode if any modifications are desired.

INSTRUCTIONS: Auto-Configuration for an Illumination Area Adjust to Your Layout

1. Click and drag IllumiTiles onto the build area as your physical layout requires. IllumiSnap harnesses will automatically be inserted, and the Effective Illumination Area will adjust to your layout.

2. Select a power supply Derate factor, if desired. (Default is 10%)

3. Observe System Statistics and Required Components as you layout your system.

INSTRUCTIONS: Manual Configuration

1. Click and drag IllumiTiles and IllumiSnap Harnesses onto the build area as your physical layout requires.

2. Select a power supply Derate factor, if desired. (Default is 10%)

3. View System Statistics and Components Required as you layout your system.
